# Load

## Definition
A **Load** is the primary operational unit in Atlas Command.  
It represents a single shipment or movement of freight from an origin to a destination.  
A Load contains all relevant operational information, including customer details, pickup/delivery locations, equipment, documents, statuses, and financial data.
